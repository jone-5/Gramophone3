About Gramophone

Music, like any other form of literature, is released into the world not only to be enjoyed but also to be shared, discussed, cross-referenced and most importantly explored. It's incredible that after centuries of making music, artists continue to combine new sounds and rhythms in a way that is original and pleasing to the human ear. How can those combinations of sounds conjure up feelings as strong as longing, lonliness and meloncholy while other combinations create feelings of excitement, joy, and even love?

Mission Statement

Gramophone's mission is to aid music lovers in their quest for sonic delight by providing a space to interactively explore music.
